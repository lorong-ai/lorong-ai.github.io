About us

    About Us

    "Lorong" is a Malay word that translates to "alley" or "lane" in English, evoking a sense of connection, exploration, and community.

    In a fast-paced and ever-evolving field like AI, the need for regular exchange of ideas and insights is critical.

    Lorong AI provides a dynamic space where AI creators and practitioners connect, share knowledge, and grow through curated programming and a collaborative environment.

    • Co-Work with Government, Industry and Research.
    • Grow your network and collaborate.
    • Exclusive programs for members.
